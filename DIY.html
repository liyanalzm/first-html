  • Washable T-Shirt Markers

after i sketched the cupcakes, i started drawing it on the shirts, at this part, you really need to be careful because pencil stains will be hard to erase. as usual, aku buat comot. after done drawing, i start to colour it. urgh this is the most boring part, because you have to wait for the marker to dry up first for you to proceed to the next colour.at this point you need to make sure the ink tak tembus ke bawah. for that, you can lapik it with a piece of paper. use your creativity, for the lengan i fold the paper into half. after it is all dried up (by room temperature) nak tau, pegang je benda tu rasa lembap lagi, tunggu la lagi. if dia kering, iron the t-shirt,neatly. then lama sikit boleh la basuh.

DIY Nutella Popsicle

All you need is :
  • Nutella
  • Milk
  • Popsicle Container

Instructions :


just add several spoons of nutella into 1 quarter cup of milk. and stir it,the easiest way to stir it is by blending it. after that, pour them into the popsicles container and freeze it for a day. then you are done!

DIY Instax Bag

All you need is :
  • Any cloth
  • Sewing kit

Instructions :


You can use any type of cloth you want, either you buy a new cloth or use an old cloth. recycle is better right? :D





+++ make sure you make all these things at the inside part okay because you dont wanna make other people see the ugly stitches right.
1. First of all, you need to take some measurements of your instax.
i just put my instax on top of the jacket and estimate the size, then i cut it. (well, i can never cut properly,as you know)







2. Cut the cloth you have marked.
well, make sure it is straight!, don't make it looks like mine.



3. Cut a piece for the infront part too!
make sure you spare some cloth so you can sew it properly and it fits. don't forget to mark the point where you must cut and also the point with you must sew it. because i made a mistake, i forgot to spare some space and it doesn't fit.so i have to add some cloth to it.
*to make it fits, while marking the infront part, make sure mark it when your instax is on the cloth too, so it will be accurate.



4. Attach the ends and sew it!
first of all, sew the back part and the front part first!



sew it twice okay! so it's strong! but if you're using a sewing machine (lucky you) it's up to you.
then when it is done, mine looks like this;



5. Fold the sides and sew it!
i told you to leave some space for the sides,right? so for this one, you need to fold it and sew it on the on of the folded part, neatly and do it twice too. secure.







6. Test it!
Once you have sew both sides, test it with your instax! mine fits well(after i added some extension,lol).



7. Close it and estimate the button's location
i fold the upper part because 1, it is too long 2, i just want it to be thick so it wont be thorn easily whenever i'm try to button it.



8. Take a felt paper and attach it to the part where you want to sew the button
this is to make the surface more thicker.





9. Attach the button!
*make sure the button is in the middle!
(i used my DIY button, i make it with polymer clay)







10. Make a hole for the button
it's a bit tricky, make sure you make the whole just at the right size, not too big and not too small. and after make that hole, make a little stitch around it so it wont tear apart later.
YOU ARE DONE!

DIY Silhouette Image

All you need is :
  • Canvas/Paper
  • Image to make it silhouette
  • Acryllic Colors
  • Brushes

Instructions :


the first thing you need is to find a picture, save and print it.
it's up to you what size you want it to be as long as it fits in your canvas / art journal.
then simply cut the picture along a solid edges, there is no need to cut the picture in detail like the hair ko potong satu satu,tak payah.
then, put the picture on your canvas/art journal and draw a light line along the corner (tekap). just like i did to mine.





then take a dark,solid colour and paint either for the background or the silhouette part, for me i use black as the background and white as the silhouette.



*you need to find an image that suits for a silhouette, not like mine tho.
goodluck!
